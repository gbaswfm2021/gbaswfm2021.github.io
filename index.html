Greater Bay Area Symposium for Wave Functional Materials
大湾区超构材料与微纳光学专题研讨会

Oct 16 - 17, 2021

Register for attendance
The 3rd Greater Bay Area Symposium for Wave Functional Materials will be held in Hong Kong this year on 16-17 Oct, 2021. As the Greater Bay Area of China have ever increasing number of researchers in the area of metamaterials and nanophotonics, we are delighted to have the opportunity to gather and have more discussions, deepening the collaborations between universities and research institutes in this exciting area.
